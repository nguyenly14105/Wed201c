Famous landmarks of Ha Giang

Vuong family mansion Ha Giang

The Vuong family mansion was built according to the criteria and requirements of Vuong Chinh Duc. First of all, it had to ensure the defense function. Therefore, the surrounding wall was built with large stone slabs, cemented tightly, tightly, high, and bulletproof. The wall was 60-80 cm thick, 2.5-3 m high. Two blue stone guard towers located on the two corners of the rear wall of the palace had loopholes, a wide range of observation, making it easy to detect intruders from both sides and behind.
